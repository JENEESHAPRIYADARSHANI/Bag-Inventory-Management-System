                           DELETE RULES
================================================================================

CAN DELETE (Admin Only):
  ✅ DRAFT     - Not yet sent to customer
  ✅ REJECTED  - Customer rejected

CANNOT DELETE:
  ❌ SENT      - Sent to customer (must reject first)
  ❌ ACCEPTED  - Customer accepted
  ❌ CONVERTED - Linked to order

================================================================================
                           API ENDPOINTS
================================================================================

Base URL: http://localhost:8080/api/quotations

GET    /health                    Health check
GET    /products                  Get products (with fallback)
POST   /                          Create quotation (DRAFT)
GET    /                          Get all quotations
GET    /{id}                      Get quotation by ID
GET    /search?email=             Search by email
PUT    /{id}/send                 Update & send (DRAFT → SENT)
PUT    /{id}/accept               Accept (SENT → ACCEPTED)
PUT    /{id}/reject               Reject (SENT → REJECTED)
POST   /{id}/convert              Convert (ACCEPTED → CONVERTED)
DELETE /{id}                      Delete (DRAFT/REJECTED only)
GET    /status/{id}               Get status details
GET    /orders                    Get all orders (proxy)
GET    /orders/by-email?email=    Get orders by email (proxy)
